About Me

My name is Waseb, and I’m a Master’s student at Northwestern studying data science with a focus in artificial intelligence. I also received my undergraduate at University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. If I had to describe myself in a couple of words, I’d describe myself as someone who is motivated, has a strong work ethic, and a passion for learning. I truly have a love and appreciation for data science and analytics. I’m constantly learning more about data science and building my skill set .I've taken classes on subjects such as machine learning, simulation modeling, and database management systems . One of my main goals going into the program was to learn skills that would allow me to help others, and I’m constantly striving to do just that!
